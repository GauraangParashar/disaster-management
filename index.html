Volunteer In times of need

Volunteering gives you an opportunity to change people's lives, including your own. It gives you the satisfaction of playing a role in someone else's life, helping people who may not be able to help themselves. Volunteering is a way of giving back to your community while developing important social skills, and gaining valuable work experience all at the same time.Rapid Response building a network of 5000 committed volunteers for disaster relief and recovery efforts in India.
